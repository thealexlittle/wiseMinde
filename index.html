What is WiseMinde

WiseMinde inc. was founded by Johnathan Gibbs and Alexander Little solely to show the world the musical talent that lies within it. Now they are open to showing off all forms of art. WiseMinde's artistry as a company is still growing and will continue to grow. As of today, Johnathan Gibbs (better known by his stage name "Artystik") is a musical artist that is promoting the company through his music, but Alex and Johnathan came together and decided not to put all the weight on just one artist or one type of creative outlet. They've since made WiseMinde an open company so other artists of all kinds can interact, bring together, and build the company with their own unique skills and talents. This came from a revelation Artystik had early in his career when he realized that the best way to link with other artists was to become a fan so, being fans of not only our creatives but also creatives everywhere is one of WiseMinde’s guiding principles. Only by working together can the company become great. Stay tuned for what happens next.
